# Occupancy Assumptions in Building Energy Codes

This repository contains occupancy-related assumptions from 14 international building energy codes, compiled in both Excel and IDF formats for comparative analysis and simulation.

## Files

- `Occupancy_Assumptions_EnergyCodes.xlsx`:  
  Includes occupancy density, ventilation rates, and hourly schedules for occupancy, lighting, equipment, cooling, and heating setpoints.

- `Occupancy_Schedules_EnergyCodes.idf`:  
  Contains the hourly schedules and setpoints in EnergyPlus format (`Schedule:Compact`).

## Covered Codes

Australia, Brazil, Canada, Switzerland, China, Germany, Denmark, England, France, India, Norway, New Zealand, Singapore, United States
